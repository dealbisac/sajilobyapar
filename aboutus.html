Background Information

Sajilo Byapar is the online based market that helps people in finding their needs and sell their products. Sajilo Byapar is the project developed by Vine Software Innovation Company under the budget of DL & Son's (Pvt.) Ltd to help the people in the remote area to find the market quickly and sell their product effectively and efficiently.
