Introduction

Sajilo Byapar is the online based market specially designed for the remote areas where the people of that area can sell their service and commodities via the internet. It helps the people to find the market to sell their commodities from the seller side and get the product and service from the customer side. When the seller wants to the sell his product and service, then s/he simply upload his/her product and it's detail in the website and the person who wants to buy the product can contact him/her and in this way the product gets appropriate marketand the buyer and the buyer also gets benefit to compare the rates and quality of the product and buy it. Sajilo Byapar is really helpful in various way to manage the time and customer for the buyer.

Selling the Product

For Selling the product the seller can post their product detail in the websites and wait for their buyer. This the facility that the seller can get the lots of buyer by sitting in their home. It is web based so one can acess the information content at all time. It is the fact that lots of people spend their most of the time in finding the buyer and gets small amount of money. So this web centric site helps th people to minimize the risk of not finding the market and buyer and also ensure the their product get the best rates among the buyer.

Buying the Product

For buying the product, buyer simply log in the website and checks the product detail and description and contacts the buyer in order to buy and fix the rates. In this way the buyer has also the option to choose among the best product and get the best product by comparing it.
